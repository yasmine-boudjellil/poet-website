About Evelyna R. Wren

"Poetry is my soul's music, an eternal dance with words."

Born with a heart that beats in verses, Evelyna R. Wren has spent her life capturing the beauty of fleeting moments. Her poetry is an embrace of love, loss, and the quiet magic of nature.

Her works are deeply inspired by the moonlit skies, whispered winds, and the timeless stories of the heart. She believes in the power of words to heal, to soothe, and to bind hearts across time and space.

Poetry of the Week

Written for You:

I try to write about the world

but every word curves back to you.

Every metaphor bends

every line feels the weight of you.

...

I thought I could write of skies,

of oceans, of things bigger than us,

but all I see is your shadow in the waves,

your face in the stars. ,

...

I’m lost in this pull,

this ache I can’t define—,

like the tide dragged me under,

like I’m caught between letting go and holding on,

...

Do you feel it too?

The waiting, the wanting, the wondering?

Do you ever find me in the spaces,

where silence speaks louder than feelings?

...

Because I’ve been writing,

and darling, I know this by heart,

every poem I write, every breath I take,

will always end with you

...
